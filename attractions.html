Top Tourist Attractions in Egypt

Great Pyramid of Giza

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in the Giza pyramid complex.

Karnak Temple

The Karnak Temple Complex is a vast mix of decayed temples and other buildings near Luxor.

Valley of the Kings

The Valley of the Kings is a valley where tombs were constructed for pharaohs and nobles.

Abu Simbel Temples

The Abu Simbel temples are two massive rock-cut temples in southern Egypt.
